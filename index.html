Rome

Rome today is a popular tourist destination, due to its archaeological and artistical significance, as well as its unique traditions, its panoramic views, and its parks. Among the most significant resources: plenty of museums – (Capitoline Museums, the Vatican Museums, Galleria Borghese, and a great many others)—aqueducts, fountains, churches, palaces, historical buildings, the monuments and ruins of the Roman Forum, and the Catacombs. Rome is the 2nd most visited city in the EU, after Paris, and receives an average of 7–10 million tourists a year, which sometimes doubles on holy years.

Cluj-Napoca

Cluj-Napoca is the second-largest city in Romania and is located in the Transylvania region. The city is home to a number of historical sites, including the Cluj-Napoca Cathedral, the Cluj-Napoca Opera House, and the Cluj-Napoca Museum of Art. Cluj-Napoca is also a popular destination for outdoor activities, such as hiking, biking, and skiing.
